# Target Company Pitch Matrix

Date: `2026-03-29`

Use this as the fastest way to realign your story before an application, recruiter screen, or interview.

## Snowflake Korea

- **Best title to target:** Solutions Engineer / Associate SE
- **30-second pitch:** I build governed analytics systems that make data platforms usable by real business teams. Nexus-Hive, Lakehouse Contract Lab, and Enterprise LLM Adoption Kit show warehouse-aware product design, governance, and delivery judgment rather than chat-only demos.
- **Top proof:** `Nexus-Hive`, `lakehouse-contract-lab`, `enterprise-llm-adoption-kit`
- **Do not lead with:** generic LLM hype or pure model tuning
- **Caveat to preempt:** direct pre-sales history is limited, but the portfolio already functions like demo and POC material

## Databricks Korea

- **Best title to target:** Solutions Architect / Associate SA / Product Adoption Architect
- **30-second pitch:** My strongest Databricks story is lakehouse and AI together: Delta, Unity Catalog, MLflow, and governed agent workflows. I can connect platform architecture to customer value, not just explain product features.
- **Top proof:** `lakehouse-contract-lab`, `Nexus-Hive`, `enterprise-llm-adoption-kit`
- **Do not lead with:** certification count alone
- **Caveat to preempt:** customer-facing SA experience is still growing, but architecture depth is already strong

## Palantir Korea

- **Best title to target:** Forward Deployed Software Engineer / Deployment Strategist
- **30-second pitch:** I build software for messy operational environments where evidence, ownership, review, and action all matter. My best work is closest to field engineering: ambiguous workflows, high-trust delivery, and systems that help users make decisions.
- **Top proof:** `AegisOps`, `memory-test-master-change-gate`, `ops-reliability-workbench`, `regulated-case-workbench`
- **Do not lead with:** generic software engineering or generic AI engineer framing
- **Caveat to preempt:** large-customer deployment history is lighter than the ideal bar, but work shape and judgment are unusually aligned

## OpenAI Seoul

- **Best title to target:** AI Deployment Engineer / Solutions Engineer / Solutions Architect
- **30-second pitch:** I build applied AI systems that are reliable enough for real workflows. My strongest value is deployment trust: runtime reliability, evaluation-aware architecture, and enterprise rollout patterns that make AI systems usable under scrutiny.
- **Top proof:** `stage-pilot`, `AegisOps`, `enterprise-llm-adoption-kit`

- **Caveat to preempt:** strongest fit is Technical Success and deployment, not core research

## Anthropic Seoul

- **30-second pitch:** I help turn GenAI enthusiasm into deployment-safe architecture. My strongest work is where runtime reliability, governance, structured data access, and customer-facing system design all have to come together.
