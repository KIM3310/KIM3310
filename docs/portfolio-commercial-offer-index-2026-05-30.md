## Operating Rule

- Active repositories are the outbound and sales-call front door.
- Supporting repositories are add-ons, optional domain proof, or narrow revival packages.
- Archived repositories are not sold as maintained products; sell only refreshed workshops, diagnostics, templates, or revival scopes.
- Every offer starts with proof and boundaries before price.
