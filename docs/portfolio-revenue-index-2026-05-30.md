## Operating Standard

- B2B projects should start with a paid diagnostic, workshop, or fixed-scope pilot before any larger buildout.
- B2C projects should prove first-session value before subscriptions, ads, or paid packs.
- Ad-supported projects should keep ads away from sensitive, high-attention, or safety-critical moments.
- Portfolio and lead-generation projects should route visitors into a specific problem lane instead of a generic contact request.
- Every project should keep evidence, boundaries, and pricing assumptions visible enough that a buyer can trust the next step.
